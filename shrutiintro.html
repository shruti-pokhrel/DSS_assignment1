About Myself



My name is Shruti Pokhrel and am currently a 11th standard student studying at Sifal Secondary School. I graduated from Alpine Academy with decent grades. I was born and raised in Kathmandu in a nuclear family of five. I’ve two siblings-a brother and a sister.
Since my early childhood, I was always taught to be a principled person. To be honest, it’s difficult for me to make friends but once I make friends, it’s great and people find me different. I define myself as an introvert who don’t like to open up in front of people easily. I am sympathetic to the feeling of others, as well as sensitive of other people’s perception of me. This sensitivity to other’s opinions makes me more self-conscious of myself. However, my siblings have always been my best friends. I always have a great time with them. They have always taught and reminded me that everything happens for a reason and one should always find the best way to cope with it. Cooking has always been my passion. I think cooking is the best way of adding some flavors to one’s life. Moreover, my hobbies include photography, learning languages and listening to poetry.
Right now I’m just focusing on my studies and getting prepared for the ups and downs I might have to face in the days to come. But my ultimate aim is to be a self- reliant and remain an optimistic girl.
